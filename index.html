My Favorite Place to go is India

Vinay Yerramsetti



When you go to India there are many things to do there.

India is a great country to visit because you can visit the great cities, learn about all the cultures and religions, and eat all the food. When you go to India you should vist the major cities and the landmarks there. You can learn about the culture in each city and eat the different foods.You can visit the beautiful temples and learn about Hinduism, Buddhism and other religions in India. The landmarks have many decents from different countries and religions. For example, the Taj Mahal has a mughal design which is also an Islamic design made for the wife of the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan. The food for the most part will depend on where you are in India. Indians love spicy food and if your a visitor of the country be prepared to eat very spicy food.
